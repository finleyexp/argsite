The Pu'Shing Bhu'Tons Series

The Pu'Shing Bhu'Tons Series is a post-apocalyptic/sci-fi/fantasy written by Monique Finley.

Pir8s live in abandoned ports sprinkled along the southern edge of Poterits Don and Dan, and Montis, where the Soveriegn Sea meets the formerly Unified Poterits.

The most famous of all is Port Askance in Poterit Dan. Only the bravest Pir8s call Port Askance home port. You have to be brave to share a town with raging Versipisces.
